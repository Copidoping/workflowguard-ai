# WorkflowGuard AI Templates

These templates are educational examples for safer n8n automation patterns.

They are designed to demonstrate reviewable workflow structure, not to be copied into production unchanged.

## Included Templates

- `n8n/webhook-validation-pattern.json`
- `n8n/http-retry-fallback-pattern.json`
- `n8n/ai-structured-output-pattern.json`

## Safety Notes

- No credentials are included.
- No real endpoints are included.
- No real personal data is included.
- Review every node before production use.
- Run WorkflowGuard AI after adapting a template to your own workflow.

A future template store may build on these patterns with richer examples, documentation, and client handoff notes.
