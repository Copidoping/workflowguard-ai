# Waitlist Activation Requirements

WorkflowGuard AI has a public waitlist page, but it does not collect email addresses yet. Activation requires a privacy and operations decision first.

## Required Before Collection

- Publish a clear privacy notice for email collection.
- Choose an email provider or backend deliberately.
- Enable double opt-in where possible.
- Define a data retention policy.
- Provide a clear unsubscribe path.
- Keep workflow data separate from email addresses at the MVP stage.
- Do not tie uploaded workflow files, report contents, or audit results to email addresses without explicit consent and a documented storage policy.

## Provider Options To Evaluate Later

- Buttondown
- Loops
- ConvertKit
- Lemon Squeezy waitlist
- Custom backend

## Recommendation

Do not activate email collection until privacy/legal copy, retention rules, provider choice, and operational ownership are ready.
