# Pricing

## MVP

Price: free.

The MVP has no billing integration and no payment provider connection.

## Candidate Future Plans

### Free

- Limited local audits.
- No saved report history.
- Community-focused workflow checks.

### Pro

- Saved reports.
- PDF export.
- More detailed remediation guidance.
- Re-audit history.

### Team

- Shared workspace.
- Reviewer comments.
- Policy templates.
- Team-level risk dashboard.
