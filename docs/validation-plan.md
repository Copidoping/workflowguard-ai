# Validation Plan

## Useful Signal

WorkflowGuard AI is useful if builders say the report finds issues they would fix before production, client handoff, or team review.

## Soft Launch Metrics

- Visitors who click `Run free audit`.
- Users who complete a sample or real workflow audit.
- Users who view the example report.
- Qualitative feedback from n8n builders and automation consultants.

## Activation Metric

A user is activated when they upload a workflow JSON and reach a report page with at least one understood finding or checklist item.

## Waitlist Metric

A waitlist is meaningful only if people join after seeing the report experience, not just from the landing page headline.

## Conversion Metric

Early conversion signal can be measured by users asking for export, saved history, template packs, or client-ready report formatting.

## Kill Or Change Criteria

Change the product direction if:

- Users do not understand the report.
- Users do not trust heuristic findings.
- n8n builders do not see enough production risk in their workflows.
- Consultants do not want to attach reports to handoff.
- The most requested feature is unrelated to workflow readiness.

## First 30-Day Plan

1. Deploy the public MVP.
2. Share with n8n builders and automation consultants.
3. Collect qualitative feedback from at least 10 users.
4. Track which findings users understand and act on.
5. Publish three educational templates.
6. Prepare one client-ready sample report.
7. Decide whether paid export, template packs, or saved report history is the next monetization test.
