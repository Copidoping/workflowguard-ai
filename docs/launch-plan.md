# Launch Plan

## Pre-Launch Checklist

- Confirm typecheck, tests, lint, build, and E2E pass.
- Push a privacy-clean repository.
- Deploy the MVP to Vercel.
- Smoke test all sample workflows in production.
- Add terms, privacy policy, and support contact before paid launch.
- Keep auth, payments, analytics, and external persistence out until the audit loop is validated.

## Landing Checklist

- Clear headline and positioning.
- Explain what the audit checks.
- Show an example report.
- State local-only or hosted safety boundaries.
- Include a clear free audit CTA.
- Make limitations honest and visible.

## Product Hunt Checklist

- Prepare a short demo video.
- Prepare screenshots of upload and report states.
- Draft a concise launch story focused on n8n production failures.
- Prepare founder-safe public messaging under the product/brand only.
- Collect early feedback and prioritize repeated pain points.

## Reddit And Community Checklist

- Share educational posts, not spam.
- Lead with common n8n production risks.
- Offer sample workflow audits as examples.
- Ask for feedback from automation consultants and operators.
- Avoid collecting sensitive workflow JSON in public threads.

## n8n Community Checklist

- Publish a checklist for production-ready workflows.
- Share anonymized examples of risky workflow patterns.
- Offer free audits of demo or sanitized workflows.
- Ask what checks are missing for real client handoff.
- Respect community rules and avoid aggressive promotion.
