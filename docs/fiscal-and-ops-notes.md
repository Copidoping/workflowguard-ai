# Fiscal And Ops Notes

## Current MVP Boundaries

- No real billing is connected.
- No real authentication is connected.
- No real API keys are required.
- No uploaded workflow files are stored permanently.
- No uploaded workflows are executed.
- No external persistence service is required yet.

## Commercial Intent

WorkflowGuard AI is a product foundation from OpsForge Labs.

The initial commercial wedge is a self-service audit report for n8n and AI automation workflows. The product should prove that users will upload workflow JSON and value a clear production-readiness report before any expensive backend, billing, or account system is added.

## Suggested Fiscal Tracking

- Track direct software costs separately from general operating costs.
- Keep a simple monthly record of hosting, domains, email, payment tooling, and contractor spend.
- Do not process real customer payments until product scope, refund policy, tax handling, and terms are reviewed.
- Keep all sample data fake and avoid storing customer workflow JSON until explicit consent and retention rules exist.

## Operating Cadence

- Weekly: review audit results from sample or beta workflows and refine scoring rules.
- Biweekly: update roadmap, risk register, and pricing assumptions.
- Monthly: review infrastructure costs and decide whether the MVP still justifies local-only operation.

## Pre-Revenue Checklist

- Publish clear MVP limitations.
- Add terms and privacy policy before public launch.
- Decide whether saved reports are opt-in only.
- Prepare a support email and incident response note.
- Define when to add persistence, authentication, and billing.
