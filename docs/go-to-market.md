# Go To Market

## Initial Audience

- n8n consultants who need a handoff report for clients.
- Automation agencies shipping workflows into production.
- Ops teams adopting n8n for internal business processes.
- AI automation builders adding LLM calls to existing workflows.

## Positioning

WorkflowGuard AI is the pre-production checklist for n8n workflows.

The wedge is simple: upload a workflow JSON and get a readable production-readiness report before a workflow touches customers, revenue, or regulated data.

## Channels

- n8n community posts with educational examples.
- SEO pages for common production failure modes.
- YouTube teardown videos of anonymized risky workflows.
- Consultant templates and client handoff report examples.
- Partnerships with automation agencies.

## First Conversion Event

Run a free audit and discover at least one actionable issue.

## Early Proof Points

- Number of workflows audited.
- Share of audits with critical findings.
- Number of generated reports exported or shared.
- Consultant willingness to attach reports to client deliverables.
