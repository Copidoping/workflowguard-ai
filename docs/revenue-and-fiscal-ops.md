# Revenue And Fiscal Ops

This document is operational guidance only. It is not legal, accounting, or tax advice.

## From First Sale

- Track revenue from the first sale.
- Track direct expenses from the first sale.
- Keep invoices, receipts, platform statements, and refund records.
- Reconcile revenue and costs monthly.

## Payment Operations

A Merchant of Record can be useful for digital products because it can simplify tax collection, invoices, and payment compliance. Review fees, coverage, refund handling, and customer experience before choosing one.

## Project Finances

When revenue begins, separate project finances from unrelated personal or project spending. Keep a simple ledger for hosting, domains, software tools, contractors, refunds, and payment fees.

## Tax Buffer

Reserve a tax buffer from profits. The correct percentage depends on jurisdiction, structure, revenue level, expenses, and personal circumstances.

## Professional Review

Consult a Netherlands-based accountant before meaningful revenue, recurring subscriptions, cross-border sales, or hiring contractors.

## No Guarantees

This document does not provide legal or tax guarantees. Treat it as a prompt for professional review, not as advice to rely on for compliance decisions.
