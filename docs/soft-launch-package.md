# Soft Launch Package

## Launch Goal

Get the first 20 to 50 real n8n or automation builders to try WorkflowGuard AI and leave useful feedback.

## Channels

- n8n community
- Reddit r/n8n
- Reddit r/nocode
- Indie Hackers
- Relevant no-code and automation Discords if available later

## Launch Message Rules

- Be transparent that this is an MVP.
- Avoid hype and inflated claims.
- Do not claim revenue, traction, or social proof that does not exist.
- Explain that the MVP is local-first, does not execute workflows, and does not permanently store uploaded files.
- Ask for feedback on what the audit missed.
- Warn users not to upload secrets, credentials, or private workflow data.

## Success Metrics

- 50 unique visitors.
- 10 audits run.
- 3 pieces of feedback.
- 1 concrete feature request.
- 1 reusable template idea.

## Kill Or Change Signals

- Users do not understand what the product does.
- Users do not trust the upload flow.
- Reports feel too generic.
- n8n builders ask for import/export or fix suggestions more than scoring.

## Next After Signal

- Improve scoring rules with real feedback.
- Add PDF or HTML export if users ask to share reports.
- Add template store experiments if users ask for reusable fixes.
- Add optional waitlist only after privacy copy and retention policy are ready.
